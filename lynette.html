Overview

Lynette is a 4-star Anemo character from Fontaine, serving as the assistant and stage partner to her brother Lyney. Calm, composed, and enigmatic, she maintains a reserved presence both on and off the battlefield. Though quiet, she is incredibly sharp and reliable, executing her duties with precision and subtle strength.

As a sword-wielder blessed with the Anemo element, Lynette excels at quick strikes, crowd control, and triggering Swirl reactions. Her Elemental Skill allows her to dash through enemies, taunting them with a summoned decoy and applying Anemo damage in the process. Her Elemental Burst summons a magical box that intermittently fires projectiles, making her effective as an off-field support and sub-DPS.

While she may appear distant, Lynette plays a key role in battles by disrupting enemy formations and amplifying elemental reactions. Her kit is flexible and scales well with proper investment in Energy Recharge and Elemental Mastery. Whether assisting Lyney or supporting a team of diverse Vision holders, Lynette proves that silence, grace, and well-timed power can captivate just as much as a magician‚Äôs spotlight.

Talents

Rapid Ritesword

Performs up to 4 rapid strikes with her sword. Charged Attack consumes stamina and performs a spinning slash. Plunging Attack deals AoE damage on impact.

Enigmatic Feint

Lynette flickers forward with a dash, dealing Anemo DMG and leaving behind a decoy called the "Illusory Phantom" that taunts enemies. Holding the skill allows directional control.

Magic Trick: Astonishing Shift

Summons a magical box that continuously deals AoE Anemo DMG to enemies within range and pulls them toward the center. Great for crowd control and Swirl setups.

Constellations

I - A Cold Blade Like a Shadow

When Enigmatic Feint hits an enemy, Lynette recovers 3 Energy. This effect can be triggered once every 5s.

II - The Shadow in the Wings

Increases the duration of the Illusory Phantom created by Enigmatic Feint by 2s. Increases its explosion radius by 50%.

III - Curtains Rise at Dawn's Light

Increases the level of Magic Trick: Astonishing Shift by 3. Maximum upgrade level is 15.

IV - Watcher in the Wings

When Lynette's HP is below 40%, she gains a 20% Anemo DMG Bonus and receives 25% less damage.

V - Now You See Me

Increases the level of Enigmatic Feint by 3. Maximum upgrade level is 15.

VI - Focused Spectacle

When the Illusory Phantom explodes, Lynette recovers 100% of the Energy cost of her Burst. Can occur once every 10s.
