Lightning Touch

Normal Attack: Performs up to 4 lightning attacks that deal Electro DMG. Charged Attack consumes stamina and deals AoE Electro DMG after a short casting time.

Violet Arc

Releases a homing lightning orb that applies a Conductive status to enemies and deals Electro DMG. Holding the skill increases its power and radius, dealing more damage based on the number of Conductive stacks.

Lightning Rose

Summons a Lightning Rose that launches continuous lightning bolts at nearby enemies, dealing AoE Electro DMG and immobilizing them temporarily.
