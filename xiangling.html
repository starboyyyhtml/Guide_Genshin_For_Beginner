Overview

Xiangling is a 4-star Pyro character from Liyue and the Head Chef of the Wangshu Inn. Despite her youthful appearance, she is an expert in culinary arts and a fierce warrior. Known for her fiery personality and unyielding spirit, Xiangling is a force to be reckoned with in both the kitchen and on the battlefield.

As a polearm user with the Pyro element, Xiangling excels at dealing high damage in both single-target and AoE combat. Her Elemental Skill, "Pyronado," summons a fiery whirlwind that deals Pyro damage to nearby enemies. Her Elemental Burst, "Guoba Attack," summons her pet bear, Guoba, to deal AoE Pyro damage and apply the Pyro status to enemies.

Xiangling‚Äôs abilities allow her to provide both DPS and elemental reactions. She excels in reaction-heavy team compositions and can be a powerful source of Pyro damage in both early and late-game content. Her high mobility and skill set make her an excellent addition to any team, especially those focused on triggering elemental reactions like Vaporize or Melt.

Lore

Xiangling is a renowned chef from Liyue, known for her exceptional culinary skills and bold, adventurous spirit. As the head chef of the Wanmin Restaurant, Xiangling has built a reputation for creating dishes that are as flavorful as they are innovative. Her passion for cooking is matched only by her love for exploring new flavors, which often leads her to venture into the wilds in search of unique ingredients.

Born in Liyue, Xiangling comes from a family of chefs, and her father, Chef Verr Goldet, is a key figure in her life.
