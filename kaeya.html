Overview

Kaeya is a 4-star Cryo character from Mondstadt and the Cavalry Captain of the Knights of Favonius. Known for his cool demeanor and sharp wit, he is both a skilled swordsman and a charismatic leader. Though often seen with a playful attitude, Kaeya is a strategic and capable warrior who serves as a reliable protector of Mondstadt.

As a sword-wielder with the Cryo element, Kaeya excels at applying Cryo status quickly and efficiently, making him a strong choice for triggering elemental reactions. His Elemental Skill, "Frostgnaw," allows him to deal AoE Cryo damage and reduce the cooldown of his abilities, while his Elemental Burst, "Glacial Waltz," creates a series of Cryo slashes that can inflict significant damage while dealing with multiple enemies at once.

Kaeya is particularly useful in both early and late-game content due to his ability to quickly freeze enemies or trigger reactions like Vaporize and Melt. His skill set provides both offensive and defensive capabilities, making him a versatile character that fits well into a variety of team compositions. Whether as a burst DPS or Cryo support, Kaeya’s charm and combat prowess make him a valuable asset in any team.

Lore

Kaeya Alberich is the Cavalry Captain of the Knights of Favonius and one of Mondstadt's most charismatic and skilled warriors. Hailing from the mysterious Alberich family, Kaeya was adopted by the Knights and raised in Mondstadt. Though he carries the weight of his family's shadow, Kaeya’s charming personality and laid-back demeanor make him well-liked by the people of Mondstadt.

Behind his smirk and playful attitude lies a deeply strategic mind. Kaeya is a master of both combat and manipulation, often using his wit and charm to navigate the political and social intricacies of Mondstadt. His Cryo Vision reflects his calculated nature and ability to keep his enemies on edge, striking with cold precision when the situation demands it.

Kaeya’s days are spent leading the Knights’ cavalry, managing defenses, and ensuring that Mondstadt’s security remains intact. However, he is also known for his penchant for teasing others, often found sipping wine at the tavern or pulling pranks on his fellow knights. When the need arises, he steps into action with swift and deadly skill, using his Cryo abilities to freeze his enemies and control the battlefield with elegance.

Though he presents himself as a carefree individual, Kaeya harbors a complex past filled with secrets, both personal and familial. He maintains a careful balance between loyalty to Mondstadt and his own hidden agendas. Jean trusts him as a reliable ally, Razor admires his cunning, and Lisa often exchanges sly remarks with him. His true intentions, however, remain a mystery, with few truly understanding the depths of his heart.

Among Mondstadt’s citizens, Kaeya is regarded as both a hero and a mystery. He teaches that power is not just about strength, but also about adaptability, foresight, and the ability to control one’s emotions. Whether leading a charge in battle or navigating delicate situations with finesse, Kaeya’s mastery over both his abilities and his emotions makes him a force to be reckoned with — always calm, always calculating, and ever-present when needed.

Talents

Frostbite

Normal Attack: Kaeya performs a series of sword strikes that deal Cryo DMG. Charged Attack consumes stamina and deals AoE Cryo DMG to nearby enemies.

Secret Sword: Ice Storm

Kaeya releases a storm of ice that deals AoE Cryo DMG to enemies. Holding the skill increases its range and power, allowing for more damage based on the number of enemies hit.

Glacial Waltz

Kaeya summons an icy storm around him, continuously dealing AoE Cryo DMG to nearby enemies and reducing their mobility for a short period of time.

Constellations

I - Ice Fang

Kaeya's Secret Sword: Ice Storm applies Cryo to enemies hit, even if they are immune to Cryo.

II - Glacial Breeze

When Kaeya's HP is above 50%, his Cryo DMG bonus increases by 15%.

III - Winter Frost

Increases the level of Glacial Waltz by 3. Maximum upgrade level is 15.

IV - Frostbite Storm

Kaeya’s Secret Sword: Ice Storm generates a burst of AoE Cryo DMG upon activation, dealing additional Cryo DMG to nearby enemies.

V - Cold Surge

Increases the level of Secret Sword: Ice Storm by 3. Maximum upgrade level is 15.

VI - Glacial Maelstrom

Kaeya’s Glacial Waltz continuously deals AoE Cryo DMG to nearby enemies for the duration of the ability. This effect is triggered even when Kaeya is not on the field.
