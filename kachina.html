Overview

Kachina is a 4-star Geo character known for her unshakable presence and steady support in battle. Hailing from a region steeped in tradition and resilience, she embodies the enduring strength of the earth itself. Calm and grounded, Kachina brings both poise and tactical utility to any team she joins.

Wielding a polearm and infused with the Geo element, Kachina specializes in providing consistent off-field support and dealing sustained damage over time. Her Elemental Skill summons a sturdy construct that draws enemy attention and generates shields, while her Elemental Burst unleashes a wave of Geo energy that fortifies allies and disrupts enemies with AoE Geo damage.

Kachina thrives in teams that benefit from crystalized reactions and defensive stability. With proper investment in DEF and Energy Recharge, she becomes a reliable sub-DPS and support unit. Whether anchoring a defensive line or setting up Geo resonance, Kachina proves that quiet strength and steadfast resolve can shape the battlefield as surely as stone.

Lore

Kachina is a steadfast guardian from a distant, mountainous region, where ancient stone temples and enduring traditions shape every soul. Known for her composed demeanor and quiet vigilance, she carries herself with the dignity of one who listens more than she speaks. Where others rush forward, Kachina stands firm — the immovable foundation her allies rely on.

Gifted with a Geo Vision, Kachina draws strength from the land itself. Her fighting style is deliberate and precise, marked by summoned constructs, protective barriers, and powerful bursts of crystallized energy. Each strike is purposeful, each movement calculated — a reflection of her belief that true strength is unshaken, even in chaos.

Kachina’s past is carved from hardship. Raised amid the rubble of a fallen order, she learned early on to find power in stillness and patience. Rather than seek fame or recognition, she chooses to uphold the quiet legacy of those who came before her, acting as a living monument to forgotten oaths and ancient resilience.

While many heroes shine in the spotlight, Kachina works in the silence between battles — repairing what is broken, shielding those in need, and ensuring the world does not crumble under its own weight. Her loyalty is unyielding, and her presence commands respect, not through volume, but through unwavering constancy.

To know Kachina is to understand that strength need not shout to be heard. Like the mountains she hails from, she is timeless, grounded, and quietly formidable — a sentinel of stone in a world ever shifting.

Talents

Cragbiter

Normal Attack: Performs up to 4 consecutive spear strikes.
Charged Attack: Consumes a certain amount of Stamina to lunge forward, dealing damage to opponents along the way.
Plunging Attack: Plunges from mid-air to strike the ground below, damaging opponents along the path and dealing AoE DMG upon impact.

Go, Go Turbo Twirly!

Summons Turbo Twirly. When held and released, Kachina will summon and ride Turbo Twirly, gaining 60 Nightsoul points and entering the Nightsoul's Blessing state. Turbo Twirly can exist independently or be ridden and operated by Kachina. Its various actions consume Nightsoul points, and the state ends when these points are depleted. Turbo Twirly is considered a Geo Construct.

Constellations

I - Shards Are Gems Too

When Kachina mounts or dismounts Turbo Twirly, she absorbs nearby Elemental Shards created by Crystallize. Additionally, when a party member picks up such a Shard, Kachina restores 3 Energy. This effect can be triggered once every 5 seconds.

II - Never Leave Home Without… Turbo Twirly

When Kachina uses Time to Get Serious!, she regains 20 Nightsoul points. If Turbo Twirly is not already on the field, she automatically enters Nightsoul’s Blessing and summons one.

III - Improved Stabilizer

Increases the level of Go, Go Turbo Twirly! by 3. Maximum upgrade level is 15.

IV - More Foes, More Caution

When there are 1/2/3/4 or more opponents within the Turbo Drill Field created by Time to Get Serious!, the active character inside the field gains 8%/12%/16%/20% increased DEF respectively.

V - All I’ve Collected Till Now

Increases the level of Time to Get Serious! by 3. Maximum upgrade level is 15.

VI - This Time, I’ve Gotta Win

When a shield on the active character is replaced or destroyed, Kachina deals AoE Geo DMG equal to 200% of her DEF. This effect can be triggered once every 5 seconds.
